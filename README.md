# AtliQ-Hardware-Sales-Analysis-Excel-Project-
Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the sales and financial performance of AtliQ Hardware using Microsoft Excel. The purpose of the project is to transform raw business data into meaningful insights that help understand sales growth, customer contribution, product performance, and market trends.

Using Excel features such as Pivot Tables, data cleaning techniques, and data visualization, multiple business reports were created to evaluate company performance between 2019 and 2021.

This project simulates a real-world business analysis scenario where a data analyst studies company data to support better business decision making.

Dataset Description

The dataset contains sales and financial data of AtliQ Hardware across different markets, customers, and products from 2019 to 2021.

The dataset includes information such as:

Customer sales performance
Market sales performance compared to targets
Gross margin percentage by region and quarter
Profit and loss statements
Top selling products

This data helps analyze both operational performance and financial health of the company.

Reports Created in Excel

Customer Performance Analysis

This report analyzes customer-wise sales performance from 2019 to 2021. It helps identify the most valuable customers contributing to company revenue. Major customers such as large retailers and online marketplaces generate a significant portion of total sales. The analysis also highlights year-over-year growth across customers and helps identify high-growth accounts.

Profit and Loss Analysis

The P&L report provides an overview of the company’s financial performance. It analyzes key financial metrics such as net sales, cost of goods sold, gross margin, and gross margin percentage. The analysis shows that sales increased significantly over the three years, while the gross margin percentage slightly declined due to rising operational costs.

Market Performance vs Target

This report compares actual sales performance with the company’s target sales across different markets. It helps identify which markets are performing well and which markets are underperforming relative to targets. Markets such as India, USA, and South Korea generate strong revenue contributions, while some regions fall slightly below target expectations.

Gross Margin Analysis by Region

This report analyzes gross margin percentages across different regions and quarters. It helps evaluate regional profitability and identify trends in profit margins over time. Some regions maintain consistently strong margins, while others show slight declines across fiscal years.

Top 10 Best Selling Products

This report identifies the products that generate the highest revenue for the company. The analysis highlights significant growth in several products between 2020 and 2021. A small number of products contribute heavily to the company’s overall revenue, demonstrating the importance of product-level analysis in business decision making.

Key Insights

Sales increased significantly between 2019 and 2021, indicating strong business growth.
A small number of customers contribute a large share of company revenue.
Some markets perform below sales targets, indicating opportunities for improvement.
Several products experienced extremely high revenue growth between 2020 and 2021.
Although revenue increased rapidly, the gross margin percentage slightly declined due to increased costs.

Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to demonstrate how Excel can be used as a powerful business intelligence and data analysis tool. Through structured reports and visualizations, the project helps stakeholders understand company performance, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven business decisions.
